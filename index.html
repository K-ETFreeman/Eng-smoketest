SMOKETEST

SMOKETEST
New method of testing a product/a service that was derived from programming. In IT-domain, programmers verify the basic viability of the new product in such way. Result of testing is an availability of program functionality to launch.
In the past few years, marketers all over the world began to use actively this method. Due to it, you can figure out if there is a demand for the product even before its creation and launch of production. This method continue to find use in small firms and big corporations in order to save huge budgets on the production launch of the first product lots.
By collecting pre-orders, without the product itself, we will find your consumer and prepare the product to launch.
It is the simplest variant to verify profitability and capacity of the new product to exist, to figure out if it is interesting for the potential consumers and if they are ready to buy it.
Give a possibility to get the pre-orders on the product that you haven’t produced yet, in a short time with the small investments. Rather, you can understand that the product isn’t interesting for the consumers, and it doesn’t make sense to waste your time, money and other resources on the product launch which doesn’t have any perspectives.
